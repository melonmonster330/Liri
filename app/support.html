Common Questions

Liri isn't finding my track — what should I do?
Make sure your phone is close to the speakers and the volume is at a reasonable level. Liri listens to the vocals, so it works best once the lyrics have started. If the song has a long instrumental intro, give it a moment. You can also try the Resync button if the lyrics are slightly off.
Why does it say "Wrong song?" — how do I fix it?
Tap the "Wrong song?" button and Liri will listen again. Make sure the correct album is selected in your library first.
The lyrics are out of sync — how do I fix that?
Tap the Resync button. Liri will listen briefly and jump to the right position. You can also nudge the lyrics manually with the early/behind controls.
How do I add an album to my library?
Go to the Library tab, tap the + button, and search for your album. Liri will load the track list and lyrics automatically.
What's the 10 album limit?
Liri is free for up to 10 albums. Subscribe to Liri Premium for an unlimited collection — subscribe at getliri.com.
Does Liri work with Spotify / streaming?
Liri is designed for vinyl, but it will work with anything playing through speakers — Spotify, a CD player, whatever you're listening to.
Why does Liri need microphone access?
Liri listens to your record player through your phone's microphone to identify what's playing and sync the lyrics. Your audio is never stored or shared.
My lyrics don't exist or are wrong — can I fix that?
Lyrics are sourced from a community database. If a track is missing or incorrect, try removing the album and re-adding it — the database is updated regularly.
