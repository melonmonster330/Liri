Common questions

Liri can't find my song — what should I do?
Make sure your phone is close to the speakers and the volume is up — Liri needs a clear signal to identify the track. If it still can't find it, tap Find my place again or use the track list to pick the song manually. Background noise and low volume are the most common causes.
The lyrics are out of sync — how do I fix it?
Tap the resync button (↻) on the lyrics screen while the record is playing and hold your phone near the speakers. Liri will re-listen briefly and lock back on. You can also tap any lyric line to jump to that position manually.
How do I flip to the next side without re-identifying?
When a side ends, tap Start Side B — skip detection instead of Flip & Listen. Liri will jump straight to track 1 of the next side and start syncing without using the microphone.
How do I add albums to my library?
Go to My Records (tap the settings icon, then My Records) and search for an album by name or artist. Tap the result to add it to your collection. Free accounts include up to 10 records.
What is the free limit?
Free accounts can add up to 10 records to their library. Upgrade to Liri Premium for an unlimited collection. You can manage your subscription at getliri.com.
How do I delete my account?
Email us at hello@getliri.com with the subject "Delete my account" and we'll remove your account and all associated data within 48 hours.
