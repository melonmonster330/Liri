How it works

Put on a record.
That's basically it.

01
Liri listens
Tap Listen and hold your phone near the speakers. Liri captures a few seconds of audio.
02
Liri identifies
Audio fingerprinting matches the song and — crucially — detects exactly where in the song the record currently is.
03
Liri syncs
The correct lyrics appear on screen, advancing line by line as the record plays. You don't have to touch anything.
04
You listen
Stay in the moment. Follow the words. Let the record do its thing.

Why Liri exists

Vinyl deserves better
than a Google search.

Most lyric apps assume you're streaming. They know exactly what's playing and where. A record player doesn't tell software anything. Liri solves that — and makes the experience feel like it always belonged there.

Works mid-song
Drop the needle anywhere. Liri detects where you are and catches up — not just which song, but which second.
Any artist
Not locked to one catalog. If your record player plays it, Liri can sync it.
Drift correction
Belt-drive turntables drift. Liri has nudge controls so you can stay locked in without re-detecting.
Beautiful by design
Built for the aesthetic of a real listening session. Not karaoke. Not a dashboard. Something that feels right on a shelf next to your records.
