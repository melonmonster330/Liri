Why Liri

Vinyl deserves better
than a Google search.

Lyric apps assume you're streaming. They know what's playing because Spotify told them. A record player doesn't work that way. Liri was built from scratch to solve that.

Drop the needle anywhere
Liri doesn't need you to start from the beginning. It picks up wherever the record is and syncs from there.
Follows through every track
When one song ends, Liri automatically detects the next track and keeps the lyrics going. No re-scanning.
Built for your collection
Add your vinyl to your library once. Liri remembers it — so recognition gets faster every time you play.
Designed for the moment
Dark, minimal, beautiful. Liri is meant to sit next to your turntable, not demand your attention.
Available on

Your phone, your laptop,
anywhere you listen.

Liri works on any browser and has a native iOS app. Put the needle down and let it follow along — no setup required.
